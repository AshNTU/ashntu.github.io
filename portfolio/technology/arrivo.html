Arrivo

Arrivo is the next generation transport system provider. It leverages Hyperloop technology to create a passenger and freight transportation system that proper pod-like vehicles along a track or through tunnels at high speeds utilizing wayside power and propulsion. The system is a low friction/autonomous environment, which enables dramatic increase in efficiency and throughput of a transportation system while decreasing the cost and time as compared to traditional mass transit/transport systems.
