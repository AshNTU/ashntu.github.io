TradeSocio

TradeSocio is a platform that connects forex brokers, investors, fund managers and professional traders. It acts as a social marketing tool for brokers to increase trader retention by leveraging on personal connections.

The software uses copy trade function to engage and incentivise traders to remain with a brokerage, and trader analytics are available for brokers to make informed decisions about a particular trader.

As an investor, we seek to boost the development of Fintech in the Singapore and regional sector, and to facilitate greater cohesion and mobility for brokers and traders.
